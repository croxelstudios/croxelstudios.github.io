Croxel Studios is a young indie games studio from Seville (Spain) composed by three talented people. We all share the idea that videogames, when created as a form of art, must be both mechanically interesting and subtextually meaningful, and we work hard in our creative proccess to try to get as close to that point as possible. That is our motto; we create games that reconcile deep meaning with interesting mechanics.
